Tomato Soup

Description

This hearty tomato soup is a comforting bowl of goodness, made from freshly chopped tomatoes simmered to perfection with aromatic garlic and a hint of onion.

Ingredients needed

  • 4 cups of freshly chopped tomatoes (900 grams)
  • 2 cups of chicken broth (473.2 ml)
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 large slice of onion
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons of white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of salt, or to taster

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine chopped tomatoes, chicken broth, garlic cloves, and large slice of onion in a stockpot over medium heat. Bring to a boil, and gently simmer for about 20 minutes to blend flavours.
  3. Remove from heat and run the mixture through food mill into a large bowl, or pan Discard any stuff left over in the mill.
  4. Melt butter in the now empty stockpot over medium heat. Stir flour in to make a roux by cooking, whisking constantly, until mixture turns medium brown.
  5. Gradually whish in a bit of the tomato mxiture to prevent lumps from forming, then stir the rest in.
  6. Season with salt and sugar to taste.
