World's Best Lasagna

Description

A lasagna recipe that is out of the world with just a little work!

Ingredients needed

  • 340 grams of lean ground beef
  • Two garlic cloves(crushed) and half a cup of minced onion
  • A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce and two cans of tomato paste
  • Half a cup of water
  • Two table spoon of white sugar
  • Four tablespoon of chopped parsley
  • One and a half tablespoon of dried basil leaves
  • One and a half tablespoon of salt
  • One teaspoon of italian seasoning
  • Half a teaspoon of fennel seeds
  • Quarter of a teaspoon of black pepper
  • Twelve store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles
  • Three quarter of a cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 340 grams of sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 473 ml of ricotta cheese
  • An egg
